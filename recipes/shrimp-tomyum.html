Description

Shrimp Tom Yum (Tom Yum Goong) is a vibrant, aromatic Thai soup blending spicy, sour, salty, and sweet flavors. It features an intensely flavorful broth infused with the "tom yum trinity" of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with plump shrimp, mushrooms, and fresh lime juice.

Ingredients

  • lemongrass
  • shrimp
  • oyster mushrooms
  • lime juice
